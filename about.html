About the Artist

Art has always been my language — a space where silence speaks, colors breathe, and every stroke carries a story.

I’m Anu Choudhary, a visual artist with a Master’s in Fine Arts from the Government College of Art, Chandigarh, India. I’m a professional artist and a dedicated art educator with over a decade of experience.

Since 2012, I have been nurturing creativity through my own institute — AFAD (Academy of Fine Arts and Design), an academy dedicated to mentoring aspiring artists and designers.

Whether you're a beginner or a budding professional, my academy offers a space where creativity is cultivated with care, discipline, and imagination. I’ve been teaching and inspiring students of all ages to explore their creativity through drawing, painting, and mixed media.

What began as a personal love for art has grown into a thriving art academy, where I now guide budding artists on their creative journeys.

I believe art is for everyone — and every artist's voice matters.
